# Marketing Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts to create respectful promotional artwork for the **Spectrum of
Theravada Buddhism** website: a calm, static digital library for Sinhala Dhamma
texts by Most Venerable Rerukane Candavimala Maha Nayaka Thera.

## Primary Campaign Image

```text
A serene, modern promotional image for a Sinhala Theravada Buddhist digital library website. Show an elegant laptop and phone displaying a warm paper-like reading interface with Sinhala script, search filters, and five organized reading stages. Surround the devices with a quiet Sri Lankan Buddhist study atmosphere: a simple wooden desk, palm-leaf manuscript texture, a small brass oil lamp, soft morning light, and subtle temple architecture in the far background. The mood is peaceful, scholarly, accessible, and reverent, emphasizing free Dhamma reading, long-form study, and a clear path from basic teachings to Abhidhamma. Use warm paper tones, deep brown ink, muted gold accents, and gentle natural light. Clean composition, premium editorial quality, realistic details, no clutter, no exaggerated mysticism.
```

## Social Banner Variant

Wide social media banner for a calm Sinhala Theravada Buddhist digital library. Center a laptop screen showing a clean website library dashboard with Sinhala text, book cards, search bar, and five reading stages. Add a soft paper background, muted gold and brown accents, an oil lamp glow, and a quiet temple silhouette in the distance. Leave clean negative space on the left for headline text. Respectful, modern, scholarly, warm, minimal, high-resolution editorial design.

Suggested size: `1600x900` or `1200x628`.

## Negative Prompt

```text
No fantasy glowing Buddha figure, no idol worship emphasis, no monks used as decoration, no AI-garbled text, no random Sanskrit or Thai letters, no cluttered altar, no neon colors, no dramatic smoke, no dark horror lighting, no stock-photo smiles, no inaccurate religious symbols, no distorted devices, no unreadable UI chaos.
```

## Visual Direction

- Palette: `#f5f1e8`, `#fbf8f1`, `#2b2620`, `#8a5a2b`, `#b08442`.
- Mood: calm, accessible, scholarly, devotional without spectacle.
- Composition: clean negative space, clear device mockups, visible reading UI.
- Typography: add real Sinhala text manually after generation whenever possible.
- Best use: GitHub repository banner, Facebook post, X/LinkedIn preview image,
  homepage hero background, or announcement card.
